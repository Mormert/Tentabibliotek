          Material

          Innehåll

          This course applies a bit of "flip classroom approach," which means that it is expected that  the students have read the corresponding chapters and sections from the book before each session.

          The slides during the lectures are used to revise the concepts that you already read in the book. 

          Below you find each session with its teaching material,  and the corresponding chapters and sections that are covered in the book. Please, read them before the teaching sessions. This will help a lot in the understanding of the concepts.
